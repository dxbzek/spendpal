

## Potential Enhancements for SpendPal

Here are high-impact features that would round out the app:

1. **Monthly AI Financial Report** — Auto-generate a summary at month-end with spending trends, category comparisons, and progress toward goals.

2. **Overspending Risk Alerts** — Push notifications or in-app banners when a budget category is approaching its limit (e.g., 80% spent).

3. **Recurring Transaction Auto-Tracking** — Detect and surface recurring charges (subscriptions, bills) so users can spot forgotten subscriptions.

4. **Dashboard Widgets** — Net worth tracker, upcoming bills countdown, and a "money saved this month" motivator card.

5. **Export & Reports** — PDF/CSV monthly reports users can download or share.

6. **Multi-Account Support** — Track spending across multiple bank accounts, credit cards, or cash wallets with per-account balances.

7. **PWA / Mobile Install** — Make the app installable on phones with offline support via a service worker.

8. **Data Backup & Restore** — Let users export all their data as JSON and re-import it.
